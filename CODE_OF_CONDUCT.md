# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in HomeVault a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our standards

Positive behaviour includes:
- Being respectful and constructive in discussions
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get started
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project

Unacceptable behaviour includes:
- Harassment or personal attacks
- Dismissive or condescending comments
- Spamming issues or pull requests

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer directly. All reports will be reviewed and addressed.
